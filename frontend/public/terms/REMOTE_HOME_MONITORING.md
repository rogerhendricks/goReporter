# Remote Home Monitoring Consent Terms

## Purpose
This consent allows for remote monitoring of your medical device and health data from your home.

## What You're Agreeing To
- Electronic transmission of device data to healthcare providers
- Remote device programming and adjustments when appropriate
- Collection and storage of health-related data
- Alerts and notifications to healthcare team based on device data

## Data Collection
We will collect:
- Device performance data
- Physiological measurements
- Usage patterns
- Alert and event information

## Security
- All data transmissions are encrypted
- Data is stored securely in compliance with HIPAA
- Access is limited to authorized healthcare personnel

## Your Responsibilities
- Ensure device is properly connected and functioning
- Respond to alerts and follow up as instructed
- Report any issues or concerns promptly

By checking "I Accept", you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to these terms.
