# Treatment Consent Terms

## Purpose
This consent allows healthcare providers to perform medical treatments, procedures, and interventions necessary for your care.

## What You're Agreeing To
- Medical examinations and diagnostic procedures
- Treatment and therapeutic interventions
- Emergency medical care when necessary
- Communication with other healthcare providers involved in your care

## Your Rights
- You may withdraw consent at any time
- You have the right to ask questions about your treatment
- You have the right to refuse treatment

## Duration
This consent remains in effect until revoked by you or upon completion of treatment.
By checking "I Accept", you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to these terms.
